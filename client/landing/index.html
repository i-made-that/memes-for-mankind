Memes For Life!

Memes For Mankind is a party game.

Get 3+ friends to play and make sure they have the Google cast extension installed (laptop) or the mobile app (phone).

Start the game; a judge will be selected.
The judge then chooses a prompt for other players to creatively interpret.

After the judge then chooses his or her favorite submission, the winning entry is displayed on all devices.

Share the meme on Facebook or Twitter.

Repeat until laughing subsides.

Don't Forget The Cast Extension!

You'll need Chrome, a Chromecast, and the Google Cast Extension to play.
